# Your ELYSIUM Account

You can use the Card Designer without an account — everything works locally in your browser. But creating a free ELYSIUM account unlocks powerful cloud features:

- **Save to the cloud** — Access your designs from any device
- **Cross-device sharing** — Share links that work for anyone, anywhere
- **Collaboration** — Let others view, copy, or claim your cards
- **Project management** — Browse, search, and organize all your eCards in one place

## Creating an account

1. Click **"Save to ELYSIUM"** in the sidebar, or click **"Create Account"** in the Projects panel

![Auth modal chooser screen showing "Sign In" and "Create Account" options](/img/card-designer/47.png)

2. The sign-up flow walks you through three quick steps:
   - **Your details** — Enter your name, email address, and a password (at least 8 characters)
   - **Choose a username** — One is auto-generated from your email, but you can change it to whatever you like

![Registration form showing name, email, and password fields](/img/card-designer/48.png)

![Username selection step with auto-generated username and edit option](/img/card-designer/49.png)

3. After signing up, you're automatically logged in and ready to go

:::tip
You can start designing first and create an account later — just click "Save to ELYSIUM" whenever you're ready to save to the cloud.
:::

## Signing in

1. Click **"Sign In"** in the Projects panel or trigger any cloud action (like saving)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

![Sign in form with email and password fields](/img/card-designer/50.png)

Your session stays active for 60 minutes and refreshes automatically in the background. If you close the browser and come back later, you'll still be signed in — no need to log in again unless you've been away for more than 7 days.

## Signing out

Click your **account name** at the bottom of the sidebar to sign out.

![Sidebar actions area showing the authenticated user's name and sign-out option](/img/card-designer/51.png)

- If you have **unsaved changes**, a warning dialog asks if you want to save first

![Unsaved changes warning dialog that appears when signing out with unsaved work](/img/card-designer/52.png)

- Signing out does **not** delete your local auto-save — your work-in-progress stays in the browser

:::note
Your local auto-save and any downloaded `.ecard` files are completely separate from your cloud saves. Signing out won't affect them.
:::
