# What is the Card Designer?

ELYSIUM is a no-code AR worldbuilding platform. The **Card Designer** is the part of ELYSIUM that lets anyone — without any 3D modeling experience — build cards that can be deployed into augmented reality. It runs entirely in your browser and gives you a real-time 3D preview, so what you see is exactly what you get.

Open it directly at [https://alpha.elysium.ar](https://alpha.elysium.ar).

![Card Designer workspace with a finished card design visible in the 3D canvas](/img/card-designer/1.png)

## What can you make?

The Card Designer is a **templating system**. Each project is like a "world" that can hold one or more reusable card templates. There are two card types:

- **AR Cards** — Portrait-style collectible cards (like a tarot, trading, or profile card) that trigger augmented reality experiences. Perfect for interactive content, digital collectibles, or branded AR campaigns.

  ![Finished AR card — portrait orientation](/img/card-designer/2.png)

- **Loyalty Cards** — Landscape-style cards with the form factor of a credit or discount card. Great for world loyalty programs, branded membership cards, and currency systems.

  ![Finished Loyalty card — landscape orientation with QR code and branding](/img/card-designer/3.png)

Both card types share the same designer — only the content section and card dimensions change.

## What you'll be able to do

By the end of this guide you'll know how to:

1. Set up a project and pick a card type
2. Compose a 3D canvas with backgrounds, HDRI lighting, and color tints
3. Add front/back artwork, foreground overlays, brand logos, and stickers
4. Write rich text content with custom fonts, sizing, and layout
5. Tune the card's material, edge finish, glow, lighting, and animation
6. Export the design as a GLB 3D model or PNG image
7. Save to the cloud and share password-protected links

## Cloud features

Create a free [ELYSIUM account](./your-account) to unlock:

- **Cloud save** — Access your designs from any device
- **Cross-device sharing** — Share password-protected links with anyone
- **Collaboration** — Let others view, copy, or claim your cards

Everything works locally without an account too — you can always create one later.

## Before you start

All you need is a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari). Everything runs in the browser. For the best experience, use a desktop or laptop with a screen at least 1024 px wide.
