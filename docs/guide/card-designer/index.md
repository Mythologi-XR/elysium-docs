# What is the Card Designer?

Design stunning AR and Loyalty cards right in your browser — no design software needed. The Card Designer gives you a real-time 3D preview of your card as you build it, so what you see is exactly what you get.

![Card Designer workspace with a finished card design visible in the 3D canvas](/img/card-designer/1.png)

## What can you make?

Two types of cards:

- **AR Cards** — Portrait-style collectible cards (like trading cards) that trigger augmented reality experiences when scanned. Perfect for interactive content, digital collectibles, or branded AR campaigns.

  ![Finished AR card — portrait orientation](/img/card-designer/2.png)

- **Loyalty Cards** — Landscape-style loyalty program cards with QR codes, barcodes, and custom currency denominations. Great for world loyalty programs and branded experiences.

  ![Finished Loyalty card — landscape orientation with QR code and branding](/img/card-designer/3.png)

## Before you start

You just need a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari). That's it — everything runs in the browser. For the best experience, use a desktop or laptop with a screen at least 1024px wide.
